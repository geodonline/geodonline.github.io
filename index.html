A brief history of Geometry Dash

Geometry Dash, or "Geo Dash" for short, is an indie game developed by Sweden-based developer Robert Topala. It is a game that blends hardcore game elements (such as precise jumping and avoiding deadly obstacles) with elements of music games (such as collecting coins and getting higher scores the more coins you collect) and has gained popularity online for its high levels of difficulty and unique concept. The game has developed a cult following online, with numerous user-created levels and an official website for user-made levels.
